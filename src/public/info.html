The Global Garden

The Global Garden is a data-driven visual art and information project that makes the state of our planet visible in an intuitive way. Every country in the world is represented by a unique digital flower — its shape, color, and growth pattern are based on real environmental data.

All environmental indicators used in Global Garden are derived from the open-access research platform Our World in Data. This database compiles reliable statistics from trusted institutions such as the United Nations (UN), the World Bank, the OECD, and peer-reviewed scientific publications. The selected indicators, ranging from CO₂ emissions, renewable energy share, water usage, and air pollution, to biodiversity and waste management were carefully chosen to provide a multifaceted picture of ecological sustainability at the country level.

The aim is to translate complex sustainability data into a visual, poetic form that sparks awareness and encourages reflection. The data is publicly accessible and can be explored in full via the Our World in Data division:
