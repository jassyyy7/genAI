The Global Garden

The Global Garden is a data-driven visual art and information project that makes the state of our planet visible in an intuitive way. Every country in the world is represented by a unique digital flower — its shape, color and growth pattern are based on real environmental data.

The data used comes from the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) database and focuses on key sustainability indicators such as CO₂ emissions per capita, renewable energy share, water consumption, biodiversity, air quality, waste generation and environmental transparency.

The aim is to translate complex sustainability data into a visual, poetic form that sparks awareness and encourages reflection. The data is publicly accessible and can be explored in full via the UN Statistics Division:
→ unstats.un.org/sdgs/indicators/database
